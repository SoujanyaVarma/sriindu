About the College

Sri Indu Institute of Engineering and Technology was established by Global Trendset Educational Society - 2006, Vanastalipuram, Hyderabad under the chairmanship of Sri. R. Venkat Rao. Under the same management, Sri Indu College of Engineering and Technology is also in the field of education by New Loyola model education society - 1979. The society is having proven rich experience in the field of education for more than 41 years with an intension and commitment to impart school education and Technical education of highest quality.
The institution is located on scenic campus of 54 acres on the Nagarjuna Sagar highway at a distance of 15KM from L B Nagar. The college is situated in a lush green location which provides aesthetic appeal and a serene environment conducive for learning.
